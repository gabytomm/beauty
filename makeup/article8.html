The Ultimate Guide to Long-Lasting Makeup

Want your makeup to last all day without constant touch-ups? Follow these expert-approved tips for a fresh look that stays put.

**1. Start with a Clean, Moisturized Face:** Hydrated skin helps products adhere better and prevents flakiness.

**2. Use a Gripping Primer:** A primer designed for longevity is essential. Focus on oily areas like your nose and forehead.

**3. Layer Wisely:** Build up product in thin layers instead of applying too much at once. This includes foundation, concealer, and blush.

**4. Set with Powder and Spray:** Use a lightweight setting powder and follow with a makeup setting spray to seal everything in place.

**5. Choose Long-Wear Formulas:** Look for waterproof mascara, matte lipsticks, and smudge-proof eyeliners.

Whether you're heading to a long workday or an evening event, these tips will keep your makeup looking freshly applied.
