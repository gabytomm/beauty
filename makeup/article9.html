How to Remove Makeup Without Damaging Skin

Properly removing your makeup is just as important as putting it on. Here's how to cleanse your skin without irritation or damage.

**1. Use a Gentle Makeup Remover:** Micellar water or oil-based cleansers break down makeup effectively without harsh rubbing.

**2. Double Cleanse:** Follow your remover with a gentle foaming cleanser to eliminate all residue, dirt, and oil.

**3. Be Gentle on the Eyes:** Use cotton pads soaked in remover and hold over eyes for a few seconds before wiping.

**4. Don’t Forget the Hairline & Neck:** These areas collect foundation and powder — clean them thoroughly.

**5. Follow with Moisturizer:** Rehydrate your skin after cleansing to maintain balance and prevent dryness.

Healthy, makeup-free skin sets the stage for your next flawless application!
