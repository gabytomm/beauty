Must-Have Brushes for Every Makeup Lover

The right tools make all the difference in your makeup routine. Here's a breakdown of the essential makeup brushes every beauty enthusiast should own.

**1. Foundation Brush:** Choose a flat or kabuki brush for smooth, even application.

**2. Concealer Brush:** A small, dense brush helps blend under-eye or blemish coverage precisely.

**3. Blending Brush:** Perfect for diffusing harsh eyeshadow lines and applying transition colors.

**4. Angled Brush:** Great for contouring cheeks or applying blush with control.

**5. Spoolie:** Essential for grooming and shaping brows.

**6. Fan Brush:** Ideal for applying highlighter lightly along the cheekbones.

Clean your brushes weekly to avoid breakouts and keep application flawless!
