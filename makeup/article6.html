Makeup Hacks Every Girl Should Know

Every beauty lover should have a few makeup tricks up her sleeve! Here are some clever hacks to save time, money, and effort — while still looking fabulous.

**1. Heat Up Your Eyelash Curler:** Blast it with a hair dryer for 5 seconds before curling your lashes for longer-lasting results.

**2. Fix Broken Powder Makeup:** Add a drop of rubbing alcohol to broken blush or powder, smooth it back together, and let it dry overnight.

**3. Use Tape for Perfect Winged Liner:** Place a piece of tape at the outer corner of your eye angled toward your brow to guide your wing.

**4. Turn Pencil Liner into Gel:** Hold your eyeliner tip under a lighter for 1 second, let it cool, and apply for a gel-like finish.

**5. Set Lipstick with Powder:** Place tissue over lips and dust powder through it to lock color in all day.

These small tricks can make a big impact on your everyday beauty routine!
