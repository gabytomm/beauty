Beginner's Guide to Eye Makeup

Starting out with eye makeup can be intimidating — all those palettes and brushes! Here’s a simple step-by-step guide to get you started:

**1. Understand the Basics:** You’ll need a few key products: neutral eyeshadow palette, eyeliner (pencil or liquid), mascara, and optional eyebrow products.

**2. Prep Your Eyes:** Apply a small amount of eye primer or concealer to your eyelids. This keeps your shadow in place and brings out the color.

**3. Start with a Base Shade:** Apply a neutral color over your entire lid. This acts as a base and makes blending easier.

**4. Add Depth:** Use a darker shade in the crease of your eyelid to add depth. Blend well to avoid harsh lines.

**5. Highlight the Brow Bone:** Add a light shimmer or matte shade just below your eyebrows to lift and define your eyes.

**6. Line and Define:** Apply eyeliner along the lash line. Beginners can start with pencil for easier control.

**7. Finish with Mascara:** Curl your lashes and apply one or two coats of mascara. Avoid clumps by wiggling the brush from base to tip.

Practice makes perfect — and soon, eye makeup will be second nature!
