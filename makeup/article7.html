How to Contour Like a Pro

Contouring enhances your natural bone structure, giving your face depth and definition. Here’s how to master it like a makeup artist.

**1. Know Your Face Shape:** Placement of contour and highlight depends on whether your face is oval, round, square, or heart-shaped.

**2. Choose the Right Products:** Use cream contour for a dewy look and powder for matte. Pick a shade 2–3 tones darker than your skin.

**3. Where to Contour:** Focus on the hollows of your cheeks, sides of your nose, along your jawline, and around your forehead.

**4. Blend, Blend, Blend:** Use a damp sponge or brush to diffuse harsh lines. Good blending is the key to a natural look.

**5. Highlight Strategically:** Apply highlighter on the high points of your face — cheekbones, nose bridge, brow bone, and cupid’s bow.

Practice will help you find what works best for your face — and give you that sculpted, glowing finish!
